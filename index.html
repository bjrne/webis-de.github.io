Information is nothing without Retrieval

The Web Technology & Information Systems Network (Webis) meets challenges of the information society by conducting basic research, developing technology and implementing and evaluating prototypes for future information systems. Our research projects contribute to web mining and retrieval, machine learning, computational linguistics, and digital engineering. In our team we aim at creating and fostering an innovative environment, inspired by the Rules of the Garage.
